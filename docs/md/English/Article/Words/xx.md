> **4**<br>
>
> Status is, of course, the key that unlocks every animal's ultimate goal-breeding rights. Charles Darwin named the status displays **rife** in nature "honest signalling", noting that male peacocks' **ostentatious** feathers prove to females that they are so **adept at** finding food and avoiding predators they can afford to grow big, bright, **utterly** wasteful **plumage**. Some deer grow **antlers** so **unwieldy** that they become caught in trees for days. Other animals buy jewellery and fast cars. From the forest to the **penthouse**, all status signals say: "I am successful enough to have more than I need. See my **superior** genes!"<br>
>
> 这个地位是，当然，关键是解开每一个动物的终极目标-繁育权。Charles Darwin将自然界中这种普遍展示的状态命名为“诚实的信号”，他指出，那种雄性的孔雀向雌性炫耀他们的羽毛为了证实他们熟练觅食或者躲避捕食者，他们可以提供长大，明亮完全浪费的羽毛。有些鹿长出非常笨重的鹿角以至于卡在树里好几天。而其他有些动物买珠宝或者快车。从森林到公寓，所有的状态信号都指出：我足够的成功去拥有比我所需要的更多，看我更好的基因。

- *rife	adj.普遍的,流行的*

- *ostentatious adj.炫耀的(too obviously showing your money, possessions,or power,in an attempt to make other people notice you)*

      她被选择到这个岗位是因为它是一个非常厉害的候选人。
