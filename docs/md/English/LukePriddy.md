## Day2

### Overview

Tenses:

1. simple
2. progressive
3. perfect

### Simple past(Done, Finished)

regular walk ->walked wait->waited run->ran

irregular find-> found

### Past Progressive(two past things compare)

1. I was eating lunch when i got a phone call.
2. we were waiting for the call but it never came
3. She was speaking about her passion for accounting until someone in the audience stood up and said stop.

### Present perfect

> The first one that we use is to express something that we do now or is st going on now but which began before. It began before and has continued up to the present moment.

1. She has been talking 15mins.
2. She has been walking for a week.
3. I have been playing football for 3 years.
4. I've been working overtime a lot recently.

### Past Perfect

> A someting happen a certain number of times or something happened or didn't happen before another thing that happened in the past and now is over here.

1. I had been going to the gym everyday until i got my new job.
2. How long had you been waiting before i arrived?

**Assigment**

> Make at least two sentences with each tense. The more the better.Don't worry too much about remembering the names, just make sure you feel really comfortable using each. If you do,you'll be able to express things through time easily.
